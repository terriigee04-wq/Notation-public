## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low adoption on launch
**Mitigation:** Free CLI keeps momentum, blog about use cases, email early backers

### Risk: Slack integration too complex
**Mitigation:** Launch without Slack first (CLI only), add bot in Week 2

### Risk: Competition from Notion/Asana
**Mitigation:** Stay focused on plain-text + lightweight niche, don't compete on features

### Risk: Payment processing fails
**Mitigation:** Use Stripe (proven), test with small transactions first, have PayPal fallback
